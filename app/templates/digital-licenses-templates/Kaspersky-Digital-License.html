To Install and Activate your Kaspersky Program

Please skip this step if you currently have Kaspersky installed or a current trial version.

1. First uninstall any pre-existing security software on your computer including any Kaspersky products from 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015 and 2016.
2. Click on the download link under Download heading on the first page of this email near the activation code.
3. Once the link is open click on green download button for the latest version, this is at the end of first line and select 'save' or 'Run/Save' the file.
4. Once this has downloaded you will be prompted to 'Run' the file and then prompted again to "install".
5. Follow the prompts through installation until it asks to click 'Finish'. Once the application loads you will be asked to type in your Activation Code. You will find this code near the Download Link on the first page of this email.


To activate an already installed copy (including trial)

1. Open Kaspersky – you can do this by double-clicking the icon on your desktop.
2. In the bottom right hand corner of that window, you’ll see something such as “click here to enter activation code”, “license issues”, or “manage license”.
3. Click the option that applies to you, and click the activate application button.
4. Now enter your activation code, and click the “Activate” button.
5. Once complete, click finish.
